NASA's SpaceX Crew-10

NASA’s SpaceX Crew-10 is now targeting no earlier than 7:03 p.m. EDT Friday, March 14, to launch four crew members to the International Space Station. Mission managers decided to wave off a launch attempt on Thursday, March 13, due to forecasted high winds and precipitation.
